Strategic Services Website

Strategic Services is an umbrella title for Farm Credit Services of America's technology teams; including operations, security, application development, and business intelligence. I managed the project timeline, progress, and meetings with the VP leaders for each technology team as well as the President of Strategic Services,


validated designs with stakeholder interviews, surveys, information architecture testing, and usability testing,


built the SharePoint site, and


utilized the design tools Qualtrics, Optimal Workshop, UXPin, Photoshop, and SharePoint Designer.
